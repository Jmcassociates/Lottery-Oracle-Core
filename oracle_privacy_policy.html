PRIVACY & DATA SECURITY POLICY

LAST UPDATED: March 25, 2026

At JMc Associates, LLC (operating as ModernCYPH3R and associated properties), we treat your data with the same clinical precision we apply to the Oracle's math engines.

1. DATA COLLECTION

We collect only what is necessary to initialize your access to the Vault: - Identity Data: First name and email address for account provisioning and "Magic Link" authentication. - Transaction Data: Securely processed via Stripe; we do not store full credit card numbers on our servers. - Usage Data: Anonymous telemetry to monitor System health and 3:00 AM database sync integrity.

2. DATA SHARING (ZERO TOLERANCE)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party data brokers. Your data is used exclusively to facilitate your deployment of the Oracle.

3. SECURITY ARCHITECTURE

Your account is protected via stateless JWT (JSON Web Tokens). Access is delivered via secure SMTP (Zoho) using encrypted "Magic Links" to eliminate the risk of password database breaches.

4. COOKIES & TRACKING

We use essential cookies to maintain your session within the Oracle Dashboard. We do not use invasive cross-site tracking pixels.
